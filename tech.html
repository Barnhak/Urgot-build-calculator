Short Trade
Q→ Auto + 2W→ Auto→ Disengage
proc PTA — turn off W → auto as they exit range

Urgot's bread and butter short trade. Lead and end with an auto if you can for max DPS.

The number of W ticks depends on how long you want to extend the trade. To disengage fast: Auto → 2W PTA → disengage.

Even Shorter Trade
Q→ Auto with Leg up

Useful vs champs you can't stay in range of (e.g. Sett because he can E you).

UNLESS VS SQUISHY RANGED, always combo Q + Auto + Leg. Q'ing for Q damage alone will make you run OOM, especially vs Doran's Shield or sustain champs.
GIF
E Trade
Q→ E→ W

Use Q slow to land E more reliably.

Turn on W during the E animation — it hits the front leg during the flip.
GIF
Max DPS All In
E + Flash→ R→ Q→ Auto→ W on
W on during E + Flash

Q before R if it's a short flip and they have no Tenacity — otherwise R before Q in case they Flash.

Skip the auto if they're low enough to die to R alone.

Weaving in the auto really increases burst!

If at max E range, use R before Q (short vs long E mechanic) — Q → R works instantly on short Es.
GIF
Faster Burst All In
E→ W→ Flash→ Q→ R

Can lead off with Q here too.

Turning W on during E animation procs the front leg for faster burst — but you lose the extra auto DPS.

R before Q works here too — same mechanic as the previous combo.

W Toggle
3W ticks→ W off→ Auto
→ W on→ 3W ticks→ repeat

Urgot's bread and butter — mandatory mechanic to learn. Unlocks properly at level 9 when W is fully maxed.

Increases DPS and tower-taking speed at level 9 by around 35%.

Practice on dummies or towers. Use the SOUND CUE to nail the timing.

Late game it's less necessary — circling to proc shotgun legs does more DPS, especially when enemies are CC'd.
GIF
Short vs Long E E distance matters
Point blank → halfway = "short"

A "short" E gives you time to E → Q → R instantly.

A "long" E, especially vs Tenacity, leaves no time to Q — go E → R only, or they can Flash/dash your R away.
GIF
E Buffer vs CC Cast E during the "leaning back" animation while CC lands

The E buffer separates good Urgots from great ones. Different abilities are harder to buffer due to speed.

Urgot can buffer CC only during the "winding up / leaning back" part of his E cast. Once he's dashing forward, CC will cancel it.

Ambessa R, Skarner R and Bard R can never be E buffered.
GIF
Mana Bug
Free W cast
Q → W
E → W
R → W (very fast)

If you only have mana for Q or E but not W, this lets you cast W for free.

Very useful early after a kill when low on mana — shove the wave before it freezes.

Mana is deducted after the Q/E/R animation completes, but W can be cast at the start of that animation "for free".

✦ Trading Fundamentals
Build leads from level 1
Every lead matters — whether it's a kill, a forced Flash, a reset timer or a gank setup. Once you have a lead, freeze the wave and force the opponent to walk into your range to catch up. Level timers, summoner advantages and item spikes are your tools for winning lane before you hit your powerspike.
GIF
Patience with your E
E puts you behind the enemy — there's no backing out after. Only commit when you're sure you can finish the fight.
  • Never E if you don't know where the jungler is.
  • Always use Q → E to make it easier to hit, especially with Approach Velocity.
  • You can E more freely near your tower or when going horizontal — short lane.
GIF
Range is your biggest weapon
Spacing wins games on Urgot. Stack short trades — Autos + Q + PTA — until you've chunked them enough to E + Flash kill. Make them spend resources to reach you. Never let someone just walk up and auto you for free.
GIF
Manage your Shotgun Legs
Your passive is a core damage source — treat it with care.
  • Keep front legs ready for trades, not wasted on minions.
  • Use side legs to CS — they affect the wave less.
  • Generally need 3–4+ legs active when you go for the all-in.
GIF
"The Line" — stay in your wave
Draw an imaginary line between the two minion waves. Trading across it means you absorb their wave while they absorb yours — a losing exchange before level 9. Stay on your side and use your range to poke. If they cross the line, punish them with your wave backing you up.
GIF
Wait for mistakes
Most matchups require you to bait out a key ability before you can trade into it effectively.
  • Mordekaiser Q, Darius Q, Camille Q — let them whiff, then punish.
  • Trading into a full kit means they can extend and win.
